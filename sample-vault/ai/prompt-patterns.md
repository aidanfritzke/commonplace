# Prompt patterns that actually help

- **Give it a role.** "You are a careful copy editor" beats no framing at all.
- **Show, don't tell.** One or two examples (few-shot) usually beat a paragraph of rules.
- **Ask for structure.** "Return JSON with keys x, y" — parseable, and it rambles less.
- **Let it think first.** "Reason step by step, then give the answer" for the hard ones.
- **Constrain the output.** Length, tone, "no preamble" — models pad when you don't.
- **Iterate, don't restart.** "Now make it half as long" keeps the parts that worked.
