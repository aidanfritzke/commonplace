# Old Man and the Sea — the sharks

He kills the marlin and lashes it alongside the skiff — and then the sharks come. He
fights them off one by one until there is nothing left but the skeleton. The line
everyone remembers: *"A man can be destroyed but not defeated."*

He comes home with bones. But Manolin is waiting for him, and the old man falls
asleep and dreams of lions. Defeat and triumph held in the same image.
