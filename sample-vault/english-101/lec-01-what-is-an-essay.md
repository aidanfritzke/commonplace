# ENG 101 — Lecture 1 — What is an essay

Prof. Reyes. The word "essay" comes from Montaigne — *essai*, "to attempt." An essay
is thinking on the page, not a verdict handed down.

- A report tells you what happened; an essay argues what it *means*.
- Every essay answers a question, even when the question stays implicit.
- "Show, don't tell" applies to argument too — evidence over assertion.
