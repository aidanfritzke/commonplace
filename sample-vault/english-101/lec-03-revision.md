# ENG 101 — Lecture 3 — Revision

"Writing is rewriting." First drafts are for finding out what you think; revision is
for making the reader think it too.

- Cut the first paragraph — it's usually you clearing your throat.
- Read it aloud. The ear catches what the eye skips.
- If a sentence does two jobs, split it.
- Kill your darlings — the clever line that doesn't serve the argument.
